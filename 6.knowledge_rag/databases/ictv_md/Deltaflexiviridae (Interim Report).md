## Summary

The family *Deltaflexiviridae* includes RNA(+) viruses of fungi (Table 1 *Deltaflexiviridae*). The genome of about 6–8 kb and encodes an RNA-dependent RNA polymerase (RdPR)The family *Deltaflexiviridae* was established in 2018 (Master Species List #32).

## Derivation of names

*Deltaflexiviridae, Deltaflexivirus*: from the Ancient Greek δέλτα (*délta*), the number 4, and flexi, together referring to the fourth family created from the division of the old family *Flexiviridae*, a group of filamentous and highly flexible viruses; the suffix -*viridae* for family taxa and -*virus* for genus taxa

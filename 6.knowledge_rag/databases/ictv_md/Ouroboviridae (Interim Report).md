## Summary

The family *Ouroboviridae* includes ssDNA(+/-) viruses of invertebrates, vertebrates (Table 1 *Ouroboviridae*). The family *Ouroboviridae* was established in 2024 (Master Species List #39).

**Table 1** ***Ouroboviridae.*** Characteristics of members of the family *Ouroboviridae*.

## Derivation of names

*Aretevirus*: from Aretê - queen of the Phaeacians

*Boebeisivirus*: from Boebeïs (+i) - lake in ancient Magnesia

*Demetevirus*: from Demeter - goddess of the grain harvest

*Doliovirus*: from Dolios - an aged servant of Penelope

*Dorisivirus*: from Doris (+i) - a Nereid, daugther of Tethys

*Eurynomevirus*: from Eurynome - an Oceanid, daughter of Tethys

*Gorgovirus*: from Gorgon - head of Medusa

*Heravirus*: from Hera - daughter of Kronos and Rhea

*Horusivirus*: from Horus (+i) - child of Isis and Osiris

*Ioniavirus*: from Ionia - west coast of Asia Minor (Homer's Odyssey)

*Kadmovirus*: from Kadmos - founder of Thebes

*Kreonivirus*: from Kreon (+i) - king of Thebes

*Lamposivirus*: from Lampos (+i) - one of Dawn's horses

*Mantiovirus*: from Mantios - son of Melampous

*Naiadivirus*: from Naiad (+i) - spirit who guards freshwater

*Otosivirus*: from Otos (+i) - brother of Ephialtes

*Ouroboviridae*: from ouroboros, a symbol depicting a serpent eating its own tail, alluding to the genome circularity of viruses of the family; the suffix *viridae* for family taxa.

*Patroklovirus*: from Patroklos - Achilles' best friend

*Perimedevirus*: from Perimedes - one of Odysseus' companions

*Peronelivirus*: from Pero - daughter of Neleus

*Persevirus*: from Persê - daughter of Ocean

*Proteusivirus*: from Proteus - old man of the sea

*Pylenevirus*: from Pylene - town in ancient Aetolia

*Pythorivirus*: from Pytho - Homer's name for Delphi

*Rhipevirus*: from Rhipe - city in ancient Arcadia

*Stentovirus*: from Stentor - Herald of the Argives

*Styxivirus*: from Styx (+i) - a river in the underworld

*Tantalovirus*: Tantalos - early king of Lydia

*Taphiavirus*: from Taphians - pirates of the western seas

*Telemovirus*: from Telemos - the seer of the Cyclops

*Tethyvirus*: from Tethys - a Titan, wife of Ocean

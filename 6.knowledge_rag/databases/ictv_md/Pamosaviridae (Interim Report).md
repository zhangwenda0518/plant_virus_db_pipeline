## Summary

The family *Pamosaviridae* includes ssRNA(+) satellite viruses of plants (Table 1 *Pamosaviridae*). The family *Pamosaviridae* was established in 2025 (Master Species List #40).

**Table 1** ***Pamosaviridae.*** Characteristics of members of the family *Pamosaviridae*.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Characteristic** | **Description** |
| Example | Panicum papanivirus 1 (M17182), species *Papanivirus panici* |
| Virion | Capsids with *T*=1 symmetry (diameter 16 nm), constructed from 60 copies of capsid protein that has jelly-roll topology |
| Genome | ssRNA(+), 0.8 kb (Figure 1 *Pamosaviridae*) |
| Replication | Satellite virus dependent on panicum mosaic virus (PMV), a member of the genus *Panicovirus*, family *Tombusviridae* |
| Translation | Cap-independent translation initiation, non-canonical polyadenylation |
| Host range | Plants |
| Taxonomy | Realm *Riboviria*, order *Tombendovirales*: 1 genus 1 species (Figure 2 *Pamosaviridae*) |

## Derivation of names

*Pamosaviridae:* from Panicum mosaic virus and satellite

*Papanivirus*: from *Panicovirus* and Panicum

## Summary

The family *Konkoviridae* includes plant viruses with segmented ssRNA(-) genomes; some viruses may have both ssRNA(-) and ssRNA(+/-) segments (Table 1 *Konkoviridae*). The family *Konkoviridae* was established in 2024 (Master Species List #39).

## Derivation of names

*Konkoviridae:* from the Japanese *Ukonko* (鬱金香), meaning “tulip”, referring to the host of a member of the family; the suffix viridae for family taxa

*Olpivirus:* after the fungal vector genus *Olpidium*, by which tulip streak virus is transmitted

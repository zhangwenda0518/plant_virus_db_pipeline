## Summary

The family *Ahpuchviridae* includes dsDNA viruses that infect anaerobic methanotrophic ANME-1 archaea (Table 1 *Ahpuchviridae*). The family *Ahpuchviridae* was established in 2023 (Master Species List #38).

**Table 1** ***Ahpuchviridae.*** Characteristics of members of the family *Ahpuchviridae*.

| --- | --- |
| **Characteristic** | **Description** |
| Example | Methanophagales virus PBV299 (OP413838), species *Kisinvirus pescaderoense*, genus *Kisinvirus* |
| Virion | Only known from metagenomic studies |
| Genome | Linear, dsDNA genome of 71 kbp (Figure 1 *Ahpuchviridae*) |

## Derivation of names

*Ahpuchviridae*: after Ah Puch, the god of death in the Mayan mythology

*Kisinvirus*: after Kisin, another Mayan god of death

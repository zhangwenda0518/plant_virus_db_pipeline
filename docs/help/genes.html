Overview

Gene Atlas integrates five previously separate plant antiviral immune gene resources into a single searchable browser, covering the complete antiviral defense line from immune receptors to RNA silencing pathways to ubiquitin-proteasome systems.

Additionally, 59 experimentally validated antiviral NLR effectors are displayed alongside RefPlantNLR data.

Quick Start

  1. Open /genes/ and select a target panel (NLR / sRNA / UPS / miRNA / vsiRNA)
  2. NLR Panel: Browse 415 NLR genes with GFF3 domain architecture diagrams (CC-NB-LRR, TIR-NB-LRR domains)
  3. sRNA Panel: View key gene families in RNA silencing pathways such as AGO, DCL, RDR
  4. Filter by species: Enter a species name (e.g. Arabidopsis thaliana) or genus to narrow results
  5. miRNA Panel: Browse 83,166 plant miRNA mature sequences; search by species or miRNA family
Tip: After identifying candidate antiviral receptor genes in the NLR Panel, jump to PAmiRDB (PV-miRNA) to predict whether corresponding miRNAs target specific viruses, building a dual NLR + miRNA defense hypothesis.

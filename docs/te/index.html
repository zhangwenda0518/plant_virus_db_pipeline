About This Collection

This database provides plant transposable elements (TEs) and endogenous viral elements (EVEs) from three complementary sources:

U-RVDB v32.0 -- 11.1M sequences. PLN (Plant & Fungal) division classified into LTR-RETRO, ENRV, ERV, EX, UNASSIGNED categories.

NCBI nuccore -- Targeted search: endogenous[Title] OR EPRV[Title] OR EVE[Title] OR florendovirus[Title] OR badnavirus[Title] in Viridiplantae. 671 genuine plant EVE sequences retained after filtering.
